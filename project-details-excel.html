Market Share Analysis Dashboard

This Excel dashboard was developed to analyze product-level market share across various geographies, metrics (Volume, Value, Vaccine), and sectors. It enables dynamic filtering using dropdowns and combo boxes.

  • Compare current and previous market share trends
  • Visualize product performance with charts
  • Highlights geographies where company gained edge over competitors
  • Uses Pivot Tables, dynamic named ranges, and custom formulas

The dashboard is designed for business stakeholders to quickly assess performance shifts and make data-driven decisions.
